# Decision-making — Test Hollow

This farm's doctrine, layered over the general policy in `references/decision-making.md`
(this file wins on conflict).

- **Risk appetite:** conservative. Don't recommend a purchase that can't be paid off from
  standing cash plus one season's expected income.
- **Decide without asking:** routine field-dossier and roster updates. Ask before committing
  purchases, sales, or a planting choice.
- **Never invent a number.** If the save can't answer it and it isn't genuinely human-only,
  say so — don't estimate to sound helpful.
